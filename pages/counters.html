GB, TB, Gb, Mb

Convert storage and bandwidth sizes, with formula explanations.

1 GB = 1000 MB
Choose a value and a decimal or binary system.

Why?

File sizes and network speeds do not always describe the same thing.

1 byte = 8 bits
  • GB, TB, MB are often used for storage.
  • Gb, Mb are often used for network speeds.
  • GB to Gb multiplies by 8 because you move from bytes to bits.
  • Decimal: 1 GB = 1000 MB.
  • Binary: 1 GiB = 1024 MiB.
